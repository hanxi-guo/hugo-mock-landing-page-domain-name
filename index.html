Open-source MIT Licensed.

Scanner 🏠

Quickly scan your dorm room from your phone camera and generate an interactive 3D model to help you plan your layout before unpacking.

Packing Optimizer 🎒

Using model to generate a tailored packing list to ensure you bring essentials while staying within your luggage weight limit.

Shopping Planner📈

Receive a personalized list of essential items based on the local cost of living to help prioritize purchases efficiently.

Sustainable 🦉

Discover quality second-hand furniture and essentials from trusted local sellers. Save money, live sustainably, and furnish your space with ease.

Multi-devices ✨

DormGenie is designed for cross-platform compatibility, supporting iOS, Android, and web-based platforms through technologies like Unity and Three.js

Reminders 🧰

Automatically set reminders, and adjust schedules as needed with smart syncing between DormGenie and your Google Calendar . Let your calendar work for you.
